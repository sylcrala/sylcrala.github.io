The Persistent Identity extension kit (Name TBD)

A project aimed at extending the capabilities of existing large-language models (LLMs). Built off the A.R.I.S. Project's particle-physics and interaction engine.


Overview

The Persistent Identity kit is designed to be a quick-install environment in which a new form of artificial intelligence can emerge through the combination of a core LLM (e.g. GPT-4, Claude, etc.) and a comprehensive particle-based neural network. While the kit meant to be modular and adaptable, meaning that users can implement it with a variety of different LLMs, the underlying goal of projects such as this (and it's foundational project, A.R.I.S.) is to establish and document the creation of an autonomous particle-based neural network; one in which there is no need for fine-tuning, just pure experiential learning.
